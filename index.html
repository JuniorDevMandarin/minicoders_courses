A01 Discovering Magic School

[ Metaverse introduction ] [ Sequencing - 1 ]

In this minicourse children will be introduced to a metaverse game experience in Roblox. They will get familiar with the 3D world, avatar navigation in the world of Magic School where they will enroll and proceed with their first assignments.

In order to complete the first challenges they will have to resolve the sequencing of the first blocks of code available for them at this stage: Step forward and Turn.
Moreover kids will be awarded their progress with magic powers they will be able to use in the Magic School experience.

Educational standards fulfilled:

CTSA 1A-AP-08 — Algorithms and Programming
CSTA 1A-AP-11 — Modularity



Dependencies:

Suitable for all levels. Not required to do any minicourse previously.

A02 Sequencing Magic Powers

[ Sequencing - 2 ] [ Mind training ]

This minicourse proposes students with additional challenges that introduce more complex challenges requiring new movement instructions (Jump and Wait) to consolidate sequencing in Magic School. Jump will allow moving two cells with a single instruction and Wait allows stopping the student Magic Pet when facing moving obstacle.
This time before geting into Magic School students will exercise their mind in Candy Circuss. A hide & seek game where they need to resolve pattern recognition puzzles.

Educational standards fulfilled:

CTSA 1A-AP-08 — Algorithms and Programming
CSTA 1A-AP-11 — Modularity
